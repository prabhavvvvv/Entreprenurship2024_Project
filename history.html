HOW TO USE

Please click on the Google Form link on the home page and the top to be able to understand this information. First on the top right corner, please click on Match Making. After Click start on the middle box. It will ask you a set of multiple choice questions, so please answer truthfully. After that it will ask for an optional zip code. To make the experience better, please add, so we can find the closest location to you. After, your results will come and it will give you which category you are part of and which organizations are perfect for you. If you disagree with this you can comment on the right box about the improvements we should make. To look at all organizations you can click on the Organizations tab on the top right. To leave the matchmaking process, please click on the home button.

Thank you and I hope you enjoy it!
